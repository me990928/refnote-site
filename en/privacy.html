Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

RefNote does not collect any personal information. All data you create within the app — books, vocabulary, notes, and tags — is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Tracking

RefNote does not track users in any way. We do not use advertising networks, analytics services, or cross-app tracking of any kind.

Camera & Photo Library

RefNote requests access to your camera and photo library solely to let you scan or select a cover image for your study books. Images captured or selected are stored only on your device and are never transmitted externally.
