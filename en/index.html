Features

📖 Page-level notes

Attach a page number to every vocabulary entry. Find it instantly later.

📷 Cover scanning

Scan the cover with your camera. Auto-corrected and cropped as a thumbnail.

🏷 Tag & filter

Add multiple tags to words and notes. Filter by tag to find anything fast.

🔒 Fully offline

All data stays on your device. No cloud required. Your data, your privacy.
